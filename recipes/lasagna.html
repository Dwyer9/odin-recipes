Lasagna

Picture of a nice Lasagna

Make our most popular Lasagna today!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 1 Onion & 2 cloves of Garlic
  • Can of crushed Tomato, two cans each of Tomato Sauce & Paste
  • 2 tablespoons of Sugar
  • Parsley, Basil, Salt, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • An Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Cover with foil and bake
  5. Let lasagna rest and then serve
