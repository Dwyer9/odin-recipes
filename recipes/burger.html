CheeseBurger

Great looking burger

Basic burger recipe that you can whip up in no time

Ingredients

  • 1 large Egg
  • Half a teaspoon of Salt
  • Half a teaspon of ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • PHalf a cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat
  2. Whisk egg, salt and pepper
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix
  4. From into 3/4 inch thick patties
  5. Place patties on grill for 6-8 minutes per side
